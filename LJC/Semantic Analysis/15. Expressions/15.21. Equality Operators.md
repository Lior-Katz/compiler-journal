The equality operators may be used to compare 
- [x] two operands that are convertible ([§5.1.8](https://docs.oracle.com/javase/specs/jls/se26/html/jls-5.html#jls-5.1.8 "5.1.8. Unboxing Conversion")) to numeric type, or
- [x] two operands of type `boolean` or `Boolean`, or 
- [ ] two operands that are each of either reference type or the null type. 
- [x] All other cases result in a compile-time error.
